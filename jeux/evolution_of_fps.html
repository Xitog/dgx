Information

  • Early versions of Doom were able to draw sloped floors but it was not kept in the final version in order to make the engine faster.
  • Apogee Software used the shareware business model: the game was given freely, but players have to pay in order to unlock the full content of the game.
  • id Software was incorporated February 1, 1991 [1].
